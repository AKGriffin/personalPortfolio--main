About Me

Hello! My name is Alex Griffin and I am an aspiring full stack developer. I currently work in Savannah, GA. I am actively engaged with my own self-guided education to help inform, educate, and develop myself both personally and professionally.

Defining and expanding my role as a developer and collaborator has allowed me to see problems and solutions in new ways. I enjoy being part of something bigger than myself, and I love being able to help create a path towards a better future for our industry.
